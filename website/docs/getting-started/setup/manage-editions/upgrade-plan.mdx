# Upgrade Plan on Commercial Edition

This page provides step-by-step instructions for upgrading your plan in Appsmith.

## Prerequisites

* Before proceeding, ensure you have a valid license key. If you don't already have one, you can get it by signing up on [customer.appsmith.com](https://customer.appsmith.com).

## Add or update license key

<Tabs queryString="current-license-mode">
<TabItem label="Activate plan" value="add-license-key">

Follow these steps to add your license key and activate your paid plan:

1. Go to **Admin Settings** → **License & Plans**.
2. On the **License** screen, enter your new license key in the **Add Key** field.
3. Click the **Activate** button to apply the license.

</TabItem>

<TabItem label="Update license key" value="update-license-key">

If you wish to update your license key, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to **Admin Settings** → **License & Plans**.
  2. On the **Update license** screen, enter the license key in the **Enter license key** field.
  3. Click the **Submit** button to apply and update your license key.

</TabItem>
</Tabs>
