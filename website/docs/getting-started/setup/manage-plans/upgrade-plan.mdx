# Upgrade to Paid Plan

You can activate a paid plan by adding a new license key to your instance or you can upgrade to a higher tier plan by updating your license key. This page provides step-by-step instructions to upgrade your plan in Appsmith.

## Prerequisites

* A self-hosted Commercial Edition. If you don't have one, see the [installation guides](/getting-started/setup/installation-guides) for installing Commercial Edition. If you are on a Community Edition, see the [Upgrade from Community Edition](/getting-started/setup/upgrade-from-community-edition) section.
* A valid license key. If you don't already have one, you can get it by signing up on [customer.appsmith.com](https://customer.appsmith.com).

## Add or update license key

<Tabs queryString="current-license-mode">
<TabItem label="Add license key" value="add-license-key">

Follow these steps to add your license key and activate your paid plan:

1. Go to **Admin Settings** → **License & Plans**.
2. On the **License** screen, enter your new license key in the **Add Key** field.
3. Click the **Activate** button to apply the license.

</TabItem>

<TabItem label="Update license key" value="update-license-key">

If you wish to update your license key, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to **Admin Settings** → **License & Plans**.
  2. On the **Update license** screen, enter the license key in the **Enter license key** field.
  3. Click the **Submit** button to apply and update your license key.

</TabItem>
</Tabs>
